My Project



Movie Mania

Movie recommendation system usually predict what movies a user will like based on the attributes present in previously liked movies. Such recommendation systems are beneficial for organizations that collect data from large amount of customers, and wish to effectively provide the best suggestion possible. A lot of factors can be considered while designing a movie recommendation system like the genre of the movie, actors present in it or even the director of the movie. The systems can recommend movies based on one or a combination of two or more attributes. The UI elements chosen in our project are html and CSS. For Data Handling and implementation, we are using TMDB API and JavaScript.
